Community Guidelines

1) User Avatar

  • Your avatar represents you within our community. Choose an image that reflects your personality or interests.
  • Avoid using avatars that contain explicit or offensive content, including but not limited to nudity, violence, or discriminatory symbols.
  • Respect copyright laws when selecting or creating an avatar. Ensure that you have the necessary rights or permissions to use an image.

2) User Bio

  • Your bio provides an opportunity to share a brief description about yourself. Feel free to express your interests, hobbies, or professional background.
  • Avoid including personal information that could compromise your privacy or the privacy of others (e.g., full address, phone number).
  • Refrain from sharing or promoting illegal activities, hate speech, discriminatory views, or offensive content in your bio.

3) Respecting Diversity

  • Treat all members of the community with respect and kindness, regardless of their race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or other characteristics.
  • Avoid making derogatory or offensive remarks based on someone's personal attributes.
  • Foster an inclusive environment by engaging in constructive discussions and celebrating the diversity of opinions and backgrounds.
  • If you encounter any behavior that violates these principles, report it to the moderation team for appropriate action.
